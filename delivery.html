Delivery Areas And Charges

  1. Almond&Butter.co is able to organise a delivery via a third party, upon customer's request, to deliver within Kuala Lumpur and specific areas within Selangor;
  2. Delivery rates are dependent on the area with rates estimated between RM30 to RM50;
  3. An additional RM8 will be charged per order for handling fee;
  4. For multi-level building units or shopping malls, customers are required to collect from the driver at the main entrance as charges do not include parking fee.
