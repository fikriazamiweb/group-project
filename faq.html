Here are some Q&A that might be helping you to find the solutions.




  • Will my macaroons be fine in the fridge overnight if I collect it the day before my event?

    Yes, all of our mscaroons can be consumed between 24 to 48 hours after pick-up. This keeps the cake free of fridge odours and ensures any edible flowers will remain fresh. Bring the macaroons to room temperature before serving, and keep it away from direct sunlight, heaters or flames.



  • Can I change my order once it has been placed?

    Yes, you can change any order within 10 days of its collection date. Simply email us your order confirmation number along with your change request(s). We will confirm all updated orders via email.



  • What's your cancellation policy?

    We offer a refund should you cancel your order within five (5) days of its collection date. Please note that there is a 15% order processing fee that is not refundable.



  • How far in advance should I book my macaroons?

    As soon as you can. We take orders on a first come first serve basis and have limited availability. All our macaroons are made to pre-order two (2) days prior the pick-up and/or delivery date.



  • I've left my macaroons to the last minute and the website says you are already booked out! Is there any chance you can squeeze my order in?

    We might be able to! Simply drop us an email at almond&butter@gmail.com. We will reply within 24 hours.



  • Do you make vegan macaroons? How about gluten-free?

    Unfortunately, no. All our macaroons contain flour (almond flour), eggs and butter, and we are unable to substitute these ingredients in our recipes. We understand your interest, so we're currently still experimenting in making vegan and vegetarian desserts.


  • Do you deliver to homes or other venues?

    Almond&Butter.co does not provide delivery services and therefore all deliveries will be handled by a third party, such as GoGet, Uber or GrabCar. If you request for delivery services upon checkout, we gently remind you to schedule the delivery two hours prior the event as the driver may require extra time to deliver the orders with extra care. We also recommend to schedule the orders to be delivered straight to the event venue to ensure the quality of your orders as Almond&Butter.co will not be held responsible for any damages that may occur to the orders.

    Self pick-up is also available.
