The A&B team consists of enthusiastic, friendly, high-spirited and passionate individuals with a genuine love for baking.

  1. Fondant or Figurine Maker

    2. Responsibilities:
    • Icing, decorating and packaging products;
    • Operating and manipulating various tools required to decorate macaroons;
    • Maintaining high standards for product freshness and sanitation;
    • Training other associates as needed;
    • Assisting with the delivery and set up of macaroon and other event set ups as needed;
    • Assisting in other duties as assigned

    Requirements:
    • Candidate must have experience in dealing with fondant and/or making figurines;
    • Candidate must have experience in baking and armed with baking knowledge;
    • Portfolio of previous works;
    • Excellent communication skill;
    • Able to work independently as well as a great team player;
    • Able to work under pressure and with tight deadlines;
    • Possess own transportation

    Disclaimer:
    • A Macaroon Decorator's duties are performed primarily in the kitchen area of the bakery facility;
    • A Decorator may be exposed to various temperature changes and work environments;
    • Work includes walking, standing, bending, lifting and carrying heavy products;
    • Duties also include repetitive arm and hand movements and hand-squeezing for at least 85 percent of the work day;
    • You are not limited to the kitchen area, as you will be required to assist the team in event and/or workshop set ups when needed

If you think you this role was made for you, please drop your resume to almondandbutter@gmail.com and we will be in touch with you as soon as we can!
